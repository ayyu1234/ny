Privacy Policy

Your trust is our top priority — here's how we handle your data with care and responsibility.

At STAR ENGINEERING, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information that you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website and services.

We collect only the essential information required to provide you with our services — such as your name, contact details, email address, order details, and billing information. All data collected is used solely for fulfilling your requests, communicating with you, improving our services, and ensuring a smooth experience.

Your data is stored securely and is never sold, rented, or shared with any third-party marketing agencies. We may only disclose information if required by law or to comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government regulations.

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These cookies do not collect any sensitive information and are only used for analytics, saving preferences, and performance monitoring. You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings.

We ensure industry-standard security practices to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or alteration. Our site is regularly updated and monitored to prevent any vulnerabilities or breaches.

Payment-related data is processed through secure, trusted gateways and not stored on our servers. All online transactions are encrypted and protected by SSL (Secure Socket Layer) technology.

You have full control over your data. You may request access to your personal information, ask for corrections, or request deletion of your data from our records by contacting us at business@stareng.co.in.

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (e.g., payment portals or Drive-based reports). STAR ENGINEERING is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites they visit.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page with the updated date mentioned below. Your continued use of our services after updates implies your agreement to the revised policy.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, feel free to reach out to us via email or our contact page. We appreciate your trust and promise to protect your information with the utmost care.
